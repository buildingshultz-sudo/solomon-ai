# Building Shultz: AI Automation Pricing Structure

**Prepared for:** Trade Businesses (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, General Contractors)  
**Provided by:** Building Shultz (Jedidiah Shultz & Solomon AI)

Building Shultz specializes in deploying custom AI and automation solutions tailored specifically for the skilled trades. By integrating intelligent systems into your daily operations, we help you reduce overhead, capture more leads, and free up your team to focus on billable work. 

Below is our three-tier pricing structure designed to meet your business where it is and scale as you grow.

---

## Tier 1: Starter Package – The Foundation

**Price:** $2,500 / month  
**Objective:** Establish basic automation to handle routine administrative tasks and improve initial customer response times.

### What's Included
The Starter Package focuses on eliminating the most common bottlenecks for growing trade businesses: missed calls and delayed invoicing. We implement an AI customer service bot to handle initial inquiries and automate your basic follow-up sequences.

### Deliverables
- **AI Customer Service Bot (Basic):** Deployment of a conversational agent on your website and Facebook page to answer FAQs, capture lead information, and route quote requests.
- **Automated Invoicing & Follow-up:** Integration with your existing CRM/accounting software to automatically generate invoices upon job completion and send scheduled payment reminders.
- **Social Media Content Automation:** Automated scheduling and posting of 2-3 standard social media posts per week to maintain an active online presence.

### Expected ROI
Clients typically see a **20-30% increase in captured leads** due to instant response times and a **15-20% reduction in accounts receivable aging** through automated follow-ups. The time saved on manual data entry usually covers the cost of the package within the first 45 days.

### Ideal Client Size
Small trade businesses or owner-operator shops with 1-3 trucks, generating $250k - $750k in annual revenue, looking to professionalize their front office without hiring full-time administrative staff.

---

## Tier 2: Growth Package – The Full Automation Suite

**Price:** $5,000 / month  
**Objective:** Comprehensively automate the customer journey from lead generation to dispatch and job completion.

### What's Included
The Growth Package builds upon the Starter tier by adding sophisticated lead generation, dynamic scheduling, and AI-powered estimating assistance. This suite is designed to maximize operational efficiency and field technician utilization.

### Deliverables
- **Everything in the Starter Package**
- **Automated Scheduling & Dispatch Optimization:** AI-driven routing that assigns jobs based on technician location, skill set, and traffic patterns, minimizing windshield time.
- **Lead Generation Automation:** Automated outreach and qualification sequences via SMS and email, nurturing cold leads into booked appointments.
- **AI-Powered Estimating Assistance:** Tools that parse incoming project descriptions or photos to generate baseline material and labor estimates for your review.
- **AI-Powered Video Content Creation (Basic):** Generation of 1-2 short-form video ads per month utilizing AI voiceovers and optimized scripts for local targeting.

### Expected ROI
Clients can expect a **15-25% increase in technician billable hours** due to optimized routing and scheduling. Additionally, the enhanced lead nurturing and estimating speed typically results in a **30-40% boost in close rates** for new bids.

### Ideal Client Size
Established trade businesses with 4-10 trucks, generating $1M - $3M in annual revenue, experiencing growing pains with dispatching and struggling to keep up with estimating volume.

Enterprise clients often realize a **transformational shift in their profit margins**, frequently reducing administrative overhead by 40-50% while scaling revenue.
