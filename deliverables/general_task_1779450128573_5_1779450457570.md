## 1. Strategic Foundations

### Brand Pillars
- **Craftsmanship Mastery**: Wood + metal projects with precise, elegant execution.
- **Tech-Forward Making**: Leveraging AI/CAD/CAM tools to work smarter.
- **Fatherhood & Personal Growth**: Quiet confidence, discipline, time management.
- **Community & Storytelling**: Sharing wins, lessons, and practical inspiration.

### Optimal Posting Windows (to validate against Jed’s analytics)
- **YouTube (long-form)**: Tue/Thu/Sat at 6:30 PM ET (prime US DIY viewing hours).
- **Instagram Feed**: Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun at 11:30 AM ET (lunchtime scrolling) + occasional 8 PM ET (evening inspiration).
- **Instagram Stories**: Daily touchpoints at 9 AM ET (coffee check-in) & 8 PM ET (evening wrap).
- **TikTok**: Mon–Sat between 5–8 PM ET (after-work maker crowd).
- **Facebook (Irish Craftsman + Building Shultz pages)**: Tue/Thu/Sun at 7 PM ET with platform-tailored captions.

> *Note*: Times reflect common engagement patterns for North American maker/DIY audiences but should be fine-tuned to Jed’s actual insights once available.

### Cross-Platform Repurposing Highlights
- Anchor every week with YouTube long-form → cut into 3 TikTok/IG Reels micro-stories.
- Instagram carousel or stills from the YouTube shoot (BTS, hero shots, fatherhood notes).
- Facebook posts mirror the same narratives but add longer captions and CTAs for discussion.
- Daily IG Stories recap builds, tease upcoming drops, polls on tool choices, fatherhood reflections.

### Hook Principles
Quiet confidence + visual storytelling. Sample hook stems:
- “This joint looks impossible—here’s why it isn’t.”
- “AI picked my toolpath…did it beat 10 years of intuition?”
- “What my 6-year-old taught me about patience in the shop.”

## 3. Platform-Specific Execution Notes

### YouTube (2–3 videos weekly)
1. **Video Style**: Minimal narration, strong ambient audio, on-screen text for key specs.
2. **Structure**: 
   - Hook (first 10 seconds visual payoff)
   - Build journey & problem-solving
   - Fatherhood/self-improvement thread (where relevant)
   - Soft CTA (“If this gave you ideas, let me know what you’re building.”)
3. **Repurpose**: Export 1:1 and 9:16 segments for TikTok/IG (focus on satisfying sequences or emotional beats).

### Instagram (@building_shultz)
- **Feed cadence**: 4–5 posts weekly mixing reels, carousels, high-res stills.
- **Stories**: Daily AM “Shop coffee cam” + PM recap/poll, plus countdowns for drops.
- **Tone**: Quiet, cinematic visuals; captions concise but heartfelt.
- **Interactive tools**: Polls for tool choices, Q&A for fatherhood/work balance, slider for craftsmanship satisfaction.

### TikTok (@buildingshultz)
- **Frequency**: 5–7/week; mix of repurposed YouTube + native quick tips.
- **Hooks**: Start with outcome or tension (“AI ruined this cut…until I tried this”).
- **Text overlays**: Minimal but essential callouts; keep audio mix clean.

### Facebook (Irish Craftsman + Building Shultz pages)
- Mirror top YouTube posts with longer-form captions telling the story behind the build.
- Schedule through Meta to hit both pages simultaneously at 7 PM ET.
- Encourage comments (“Show me your best metal/wood combo piece”).
