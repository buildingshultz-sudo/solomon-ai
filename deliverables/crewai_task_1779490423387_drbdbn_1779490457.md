## The 10 Layout & Organization Plays

**Each "play" will feature:**
- A title and brief overview
- A visual diagram or sketch
- Step-by-step guidance
- "Shop Tested" tips for real-life application

### Play #1: The Power of Zoning

   - **Define clear zones:** Assembly, cutting, finishing, tool storage, materials, and "messy work."
   - **Why:** Reduces time wasted moving between tasks and keeps dangerous tools away from fragile work.

### Play #2: Mobile Workstations

   - **How:** Use rolling workbenches and carts for flexibility.
   - **Shop Tip:** Retrofit old toolboxes or simple plywood builds with locking casters.

### Play #3: High-Impact Vertical Storage

   - **Maximize wall space** with French cleats, shelving, and pegboards.
   - **Pro Move:** Hanging frequently used tools at eye level for rapid access.

### Play #4: Lighting for Productivity

   - **Layered lighting:** Overhead LEDs + task lights = fewer mistakes and improved safety.
   - **DIY Fix:** Clamp-on shop lights positioned over specific benches.

### Play #5: Cord & Hose Management

   - **Tangle-free workspace:** Retractable reels, hooks, and labeled cords/hoses.
   - **Budget Option:** Reuse garden hose reels for air hoses.

### Play #6: Floor Plan Flow

   - **Efficient traffic:** Keep pathways clear; avoid trip hazards.
   - **Golden Rule:** No clutter within a 3-ft path from main shop door to back wall.

### Play #7: Clutter Control Habits

   - **2-minute rule:** Clean up between every major task.
   - **System:** Daily 5-minute sweep at end of shop session.

### Play #8: Materials Staging

   - **Easy in, easy out:** Slant racks for sheet goods; vertical bins for lumber cutoffs.
   - **Side Hustle:** Bonus tip for storing reclaimed material efficiently.

### Play #9: Dedicated Project Spaces

   - **Mini project stations:** Use folding tables or wall-hung flip-down benches for ongoing builds that need to “pause.”
   - **Keeps projects safe** while freeing main bench space.

### Play #10: Safety & Comfort Upgrades

   - **Ear & eye protection:** Wall-mounted dispenser by the door.
   - **Climate control:** Simple solutions (fan placement, draft blockers).
