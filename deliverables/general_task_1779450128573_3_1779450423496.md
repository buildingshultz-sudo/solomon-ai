## 1. Optimal Gumroad Ebook Formatting (Recommended Specs)

| Aspect | Recommendation | Rationale for Gumroad |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Primary file** | PDF (exported at 300 DPI, vector text, fonts embedded) | Universal compatibility across devices Gumroad customers use. |
| **Secondary file (optional)** | ePub (reflowable) | Optional bonus for mobile readers; PDF remains the hero format. |
| **Page size** | US Letter (8.5” x 11”) landscape-friendly margins (0.75” outer, 1” inner) | Mimics workbook/blueprint feel, prints cleanly if customers want hard copy. |
| **Typography** | Headings: Sans serif (e.g., Montserrat Bold 32 pt on cover, 20 pt inside). Body: Humanist sans or slab (e.g., Source Sans/Roboto Slab 12 pt, 1.25 line spacing). Use bold color accents instead of underlines. | Keeps text legible on tablets and quick to skim on phones. |
| **Structure** | Cover → Legal + “For Jed’s Review” watermark → Table of Contents → Sections with clear H2/H3 → Action checklists → CTA/next steps. | Matches Gumroad buyers’ expectation of quick-reference guides. |
| **Assets** | Embed diagrams as PNG (300 DPI). Maintain consistent color palette (#0B3D91 deep blue, #F4C542 accent yellow, neutral grays). | Reflects “builder/blueprint” aesthetic while remaining print friendly. |
| **File size management** | Keep <100 MB for fast Gumroad delivery (well below 16 GB limit). Compress images, flatten transparencies. |
| **Accessibility** | Tag headings, add alt text for diagrams, maintain 44 px minimum for tappable elements in interactive PDF. | Improves user experience and ADA compliance. |

**Title:** Builders AI Blueprint: Practical Automations for Real-World Projects  
**Hook:** Turn jobsite intuition into AI-powered workflows—without becoming a “tech guy.”

**Short Description:**  
Builders AI Blueprint is the step-by-step field manual that shows tradespeople, fabricators, and project leads exactly how to plug AI into the work they already do. Learn how pros are using AI to quote faster, win better clients, and run tighter crews—with zero fluff and no coding prerequisite.

**Long Description (Gumroad copy-ready):**

> **Why builders need this now**  
> Material costs, bids, and client expectations have changed faster than most shops can keep up. The crews that stay profitable are the ones turning AI into an extra set of hands, not a buzzword.
> 
> **What’s inside**  
> - **Foundations:** Decode AI jargon in plain English and map it to the jobs you already know (estimating, scheduling, procurement).  
> - **Jobsite Playbooks:** Follow ready-made workflows for quoting, design revisions, safety logs, marketing, and customer comms.  
> - **Toolbox:** Compare the exact prompts, automations, and templates Jed uses with trades teams today—plus how to customize them for your shop size.
> 
> **Built for doers, not theorists**  
> Every section ends with a “Get It Done” checklist. You’ll know what to delegate to AI, what to keep in-house, and how to implement in under an hour.
> 
> **You’ll walk away with:**  
> - AI-ready intake forms for leads and service calls  
> - Prompt templates for RFIs, change orders, and scope clarifications  
> - A pricing/estimate sanity-checker you can run before every bid  
> - Marketing snippets that sound like you—not a bot
> 
> **Perfect for:** GC’s, remodelers, fabricators, shop owners, and any builder-creator who wants AI to sweat the paperwork so you can stay on the tools.
