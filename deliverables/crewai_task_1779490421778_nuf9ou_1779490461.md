## 7. Best Practices for Shop Content in 2026

- **Short-Form Dominance:** Instagram Reels and TikTok remain the fastest-growing formats. Keep videos 15–45s, prioritize visual punches.
- **Authenticity Wins:** Audiences want to see “real” builds, honest mistakes, and personality. Don’t polish out every flaw — let genuine shop moments shine.
- **First 3 Seconds = Critical:** TikTok and Reels now weigh first 3 seconds heavily for algorithm pushes. Hook immediately with motion, close action, or a “Did you know?” tip.
- **Educate + Entertain:** How-to tips and satisfying progress shots outperform pure inspiration. Consider mini-tutorials or clever hacks weekly.
- **Integrate Personal Touch:** Subtle family/home life moments (not staged) build community trust and brand warmth.
- **Loop Opportunity:** Where possible, end some videos where they began for increased “watch time loops” on TikTok/IG.
- **Hashtag Strategy:** Use branded (#BuildingShultz), category (e.g., #Woodworking, #MakersGonnaMake), and trending weekly (e.g., #TipTuesday, #ShopHack).
- **Platform Tweaks:** Slightly more narrative/context for Facebook; tighter edits and heavier music/sound effect cues for TikTok.
