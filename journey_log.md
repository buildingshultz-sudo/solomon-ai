# The Solomon Story - Journey Log

## The Story So Far

### The Beginning
Jed Shultz is a journeyman pipefitter working full-time in construction, married with a family. He has a vision: to build a million-dollar business using YouTube as a funnel. His channel, "Building Shultz," currently has around 1,450 subscribers and 287 videos. His motto is simple but powerful: "Be Inspired, Stay Humble, and Build."

### The AI Decision
Realizing he couldn't afford to hire staff, Jed decided to use AI as his business partner. He wants to show tradesmen and makers how AI can free up time for family and creating. This is an untapped niche—most AI content is created by tech people for tech people. Jed's journey is about bringing AI to the blue-collar world.

### The Struggles
The path hasn't been easy. Jed constantly ran out of Manus credits trying to build Solomon. When credits ran out, he had to retrain multiple free AI agents, trying to continue the work. These free agents couldn't pick up where Manus left off—they lost context and progress. There were multiple failed attempts at building the Solomon Telegram bot, plagued by authentication errors, ghost bots, and token conflicts. The Tauri/Rust approach for the IronEdit app kept failing due to VS Build Tools and compilation errors. Solomon often gave generic answers instead of checking real data, and would tell Jed to do things manually instead of just doing them. The PC agent window stubbornly stayed visible despite multiple "fixes." All of this was happening at 5 AM before Jed's day job in construction, as he balanced family, full-time pipefitting work, and building a business.

### The Breakthroughs
But persistence paid off. Jed finally got Solomon alive and responding on Telegram. He revoked the ghost bot token and got clean responses. He successfully connected the PC agent so Sol could control the desktop. IronEdit v1.0 was successfully deployed using Electron instead of Tauri. Sol gained image vision, browser automation, and DaVinci Resolve scripting capabilities. The biggest breakthrough was the moment Sol started executing commands himself instead of giving instructions.

### The Vision
The ultimate goal is a fully autonomous AI business partner that handles marketing, video editing, SEO, content strategy, and business operations while Jed focuses on creating and his family. The five-year goal is a million-dollar company.
