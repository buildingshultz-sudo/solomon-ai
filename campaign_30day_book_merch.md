# Building Shultz — 30-Day Book & Merch Launch Campaign

Campaign source for Solomon's auto-poster. Promotes the book **"Motivation for Tough Guys"**
(digital, hardback planner, audiobook — 52 quotes for tradesmen) and **Building Shultz Gear**
(heavyweight tees, fleece hoodies, 15oz mugs, hardhat stickers, leather-patch trucker hats,
field-notes journals, shop aprons, wall clocks — built around the book's signature quotes).

**Voice:** direct, practical, no fluff, grounded in real jobsite experience. Blue-collar and
motivational, never corporate. Sign-off vibe: "Be Inspired, Stay Humble, and Build."

**How Solomon uses this file:** each day has a MORNING brief (fires ~7 AM CT) and an EVENING
brief (fires ~6 PM CT). Solomon writes the actual Facebook post from the brief and auto-posts
it; Instagram and YouTube community versions are sent to Telegram for manual posting until
those APIs are authorized. Use a "link in bio" style CTA (no hard-coded URLs).
